Established in 2018, Abhiruchi Restaurant's was founded with a vision to deliver authentic Mandi and traditional Biryani with unmatched quality and consistency. Our journey began with a simple goal — to create a place where food lovers can enjoy rich flavors prepared with honesty and dedication.
We specialize in Chicken Mandi, Mutton Mandi, Chicken Biryani, and Mutton Biryani, each prepared using time-tested recipes and aromatic spices. Hygiene, freshness, and customer satisfaction are the pillars of our service.
Over the years, we have proudly served thousands of happy customers and continue to grow as a trusted name in taste and tradition.
