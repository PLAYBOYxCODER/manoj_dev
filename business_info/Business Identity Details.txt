About Us :
Established in 2018, Abhiruchi Restaurant's was created with a vision to bring the true taste of royal Mandi and traditional Biryani to our community. Our recipes are inspired by rich culinary heritage and prepared with carefully selected spices, long-grain premium rice, and fresh meat to ensure authentic flavor in every bite.
We specialize in Chicken Mandi, Mutton Mandi, Chicken Biryani, and Mutton Biryani, offering generous portions and consistent quality that keeps our customers coming back.
At Abhiruchi Restaurant's, we believe food is more than just a meal — it is an experience of tradition, warmth, and satisfaction. With a commitment to hygiene, hospitality, and excellence, we proudly serve flavors that make every grain tell a royal story.
